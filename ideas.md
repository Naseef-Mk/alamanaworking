## Idea A — "Desert Modernism"

**Design Movement:** Brutalist Minimalism meets Middle Eastern Craft

**Core Principles:**
- Raw typographic power — oversized, stacked headlines that command space
- Warm desert tones cut by electric teal accents
- Asymmetric two-column layouts with intentional imbalance
- Texture and grain layered over flat surfaces

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: warm off-white (#F5F0E8) — sand, parchment
- Accent: electric teal (#00D4C8) — the brand's tech identity
- Dark: deep charcoal (#1A1A1A) — weight and authority
- Muted: warm taupe (#C8B89A) — earthy grounding
- Emotional intent: warmth + precision, local identity + global capability

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric split: text-heavy left column, visual/graphic right column
- Full-bleed section transitions with diagonal cuts
- Sticky left rail for section labels
- Cards with offset borders and drop shadows

**Signature Elements:**
- Oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03) as decorative anchors
- Teal underline strokes on key words
- Grain texture overlay on hero background

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover reveals: service cards expand to show details
- Scroll-triggered number counters
- Cursor-following teal dot on desktop

**Animation:**
- Hero headline: staggered word reveal (slide up from below)
- Service cards: subtle lift on hover with shadow expansion
- Section transitions: fade + slight Y-translate

**Typography System:**
- Display: Syne (bold, geometric, modern)
- Body: DM Sans (clean, readable)
- Accent labels: Space Mono (monospace for tech feel)

## Idea B — "Obsidian Tech" (SELECTED)

**Design Movement:** Dark Luxury Tech — premium SaaS meets Gulf modernism

**Core Principles:**
- Deep dark backgrounds with luminous teal/cyan glow accents
- Glass-morphism cards with subtle blur and border highlights
- Generous whitespace within a dark canvas
- Precision grid with deliberate asymmetric breaks

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: near-black (#0D1117) — depth, focus, premium
- Surface: dark slate (#161B22) — card backgrounds
- Accent: electric cyan (#00E5CC) — brand teal, glowing
- Secondary accent: warm amber (#F59E0B) — warmth contrast
- Text: crisp white (#F0F6FF) and muted (#8B949E)
- Emotional intent: authority, sophistication, cutting-edge capability

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width dark hero with floating glass UI mockup
- Bento-grid services section (unequal card sizes)
- Horizontal scrolling "what we build" ticker
- Zigzag process steps with connecting line

**Signature Elements:**
- Glowing teal border on featured cards
- Subtle grid dot pattern background on hero
- Frosted glass panels with backdrop-blur

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Cards glow on hover (box-shadow with teal color)
- Smooth scroll with section snap
- WhatsApp CTA pulses gently

**Animation:**
- Hero: text slides in from left, mockup floats in from right
- Bento cards: stagger fade-in on scroll
- Ticker: continuous horizontal scroll

**Typography System:**
- Display: Outfit (modern, geometric, confident)
- Body: Inter (clean, universal)
- Code/labels: JetBrains Mono (technical credibility)

## Idea C — "Coastal Clarity"

**Design Movement:** Scandinavian Minimalism with Gulf warmth

**Core Principles:**
- Extreme whitespace and breathing room
- Thin line work and delicate grid structures
- Teal as the sole accent in a sea of white and light gray
- Photography-forward with real imagery

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: pure white (#FFFFFF) and light gray (#F8FAFC)
- Accent: teal (#00BFA5) — restrained, precise
- Text: dark navy (#0F172A) — sharp contrast
- Borders: very light (#E2E8F0)
- Emotional intent: trust, clarity, professionalism

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Single-column with generous vertical rhythm
- Full-bleed image sections alternating with white text sections
- Minimal navigation with large text links
- Services as a simple numbered list with expanding details

**Signature Elements:**
- Hairline horizontal rules as section dividers
- Large pull quotes in teal
- Monochrome photography with teal color overlays

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Accordion-style service expansion
- Hover: underline draw animation on links
- Minimal, purposeful transitions

**Animation:**
- Fade-in on scroll (opacity only, no movement)
- Underline draw on nav hover
- Smooth accordion expand/collapse

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (editorial, authoritative)
- Body: Source Sans 3 (clean, readable)
- Labels: All-caps letter-spaced Outfit

## Selected: Idea B — "Obsidian Tech"

Dark luxury tech aesthetic with glowing teal accents, glass-morphism cards, and a bento-grid layout. This approach positions Al Amana Technologies as a premium, cutting-edge digital agency while maintaining the brand's teal identity.
